What is Boxed Office?

Boxed Office is a premium, self-contained, soundproof workspace equipped with essential amenities like a desk, comfortable seating, power outlets, and ventilation. It's designed for privacy and focus. Users easily pay for access via a simple QR code and mobile payment system, charged per minute of use, ensuring it’s available when needed.
